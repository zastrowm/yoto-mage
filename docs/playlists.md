# Playlists

Browse and view your Yoto Make Your Own playlists.

## Prerequisites

You must be logged in with your Yoto account. If you're not logged in, you'll see a prompt to connect.

## How to Use

1. Navigate to **Playlists** in the sidebar
2. Your playlists are shown with cover art, title, and total duration
3. Click any playlist to view its tracks

## Playlist Detail

The detail view shows:

- Cover image and title
- Total number of tracks and combined duration
- Track list with individual track icons, titles, and durations

Use the **← Back** button or the breadcrumb links at the top to return to the list.

## Connecting Your Account

1. Click **Login with Yoto** on the Playlists page (or Home page)
2. You'll be redirected to Yoto's login page
3. After authenticating, you'll be redirected back to the app
4. Your session persists locally — you won't need to log in again until your refresh token expires

## Logging Out

Click **Logout** on the Home page to disconnect your Yoto account. This removes your stored tokens.
